Siefert surface for Trefoil knot

What is a Seifert surface? Very loosly, a Serifert surace is a two sided surface whos boundary is a knot. You may think of this surface as the result of taking a wire frame of a knot and dipping it into some soapy water and taking it out. The result will be a surface like this.

This particular surface is a surface for the trefoil knot obtained by the construction stated in Seifert's algorithm, outlined in my notes. Also fun picture of trefoil knot to the right! or below!
