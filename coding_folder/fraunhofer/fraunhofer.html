Snippets from summer internship at Fraunhofer IWES

For the summer of 2024 I was awarded a DAAD RISE Professional scholarship which involved completing an intership at Fraunhofer IWES on the campus of Carl von Ossietzky University of Oldenburg. Fraunhofer IWES (Institute For Windenergy Systems) is an applied research institute which is concerned with developing, modelling and improving wind turbine systems. During my time there I worked on analysing data (mainly wind data) gathered by MET (Meteorogical) masts and running CFD (Computational Fluid Dynamics) simulations solving RANS equations over a particular terrain which I was given terrain data for.

Below is a relatively heavily censored versions of images in a presentain I gave. The idea of putting this here is for you, the reader, to maybe get some impression of the types of things I was looking at and thinking about. I got covered up he axes numbers and some images as there is some data confidentiality in my work which I do not want to encroach on.
